    BRAINSTROMING & CONCEPT

    visual disign aims to improve a design's/product's aesthetic appeal and usability with suitable images, typography, space,layout,and color. visual design is about more than aesthetics disigners place elements carefully to creat interfaces that optimize users experience and drive conversion.

    UI/UX PLANNING

    UI(user interface) design is all about what users actually see on the application's screen -text, colors, backgrounds, icons, and moving elements like animations. UX(user experience) design focuses on studying how users move through all the different elements of the UI.

    4

    INTERACTION

    interaction design is the design that facilitates interactions between users and digital products such as websites and apps. Sometimes, the interaction only involves the design itself, but at other sides it includes related elements that help users achieve their goals such as aesthetics, motion, sound, space, and many more.
